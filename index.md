# About me
I'm an assistant professor in statistics at [Zicklin](https://zicklin.baruch.cuny.edu/) ([Baruch College](http://www.baruch.cuny.edu/), [CUNY](http://www2.cuny.edu/)). I got a PhD. in  [Statistical Science](http://stat.duke.edu) at [Duke](http://duke.edu) under the supervision of [Jim Berger](http://stat.duke.edu/~berger). Most of the research I've done so far is related to Bayesian hypothesis testing and model selection, but I'm also interested in other areas, such as design of experiments or foundations. If you *really* want to see a picture of me, you can [click here](http://VicPena.github.io/mugshot.png).
